Ada Mesmer, also known as the "Psychologist"

Ada Mesmer is one of many characters in Identity V. She was introduced as part of the first "canon" couple in the game, having a unique relationship dynamic with her husband Emil Mesmer. Ada was first introduced into the game in 2021, along with Emil. Ada Mesmer served as Emil Mesmer's psychologist for the time he spent in a mental asylum. Eventually, Ada and Emil fled the asylum together, searching for a "paradise" where both of them could be happy and Ada could conduct experiments on Emil Mesmer.

Emil Mesmer, also known as the "Patient"

Emil Mesmer is one of many characters in Identity V. He was introduced as part of the first "canon" couple in the game, having a unique relationship dynamic with his wife Ada Mesmer. Emil was first introduced into the game in 2021, along with Ada. Emil Mesmer served as Ada Mesmer's patient for the time she worked in a mental asylum. Eventually, Emil and Ada fled the asylum together, searching for a "paradise" where both of them could be happy and Ada Mesmer could conduct experiments on Emil.
